An Artist Can't Hide Behind The Truth


Explanation (if you want it) ▽

"A scientist can pretend that his work isn't himself, it's merely the impersonal truth. An artist can't hide behind the truth. He can't hide anywhere." Ursula K. LeGuin, The Dispossessed

The letters of the title of this piece is encoded in its stripes as a hidden message from the hiding artist.
